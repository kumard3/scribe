# Scribe — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-14_

Scribe is a speech-to-text app that runs **on your device**. We do not operate
a server, we have no account system, and we do not collect, sell, or share your
personal data. There is no analytics SDK, no advertising, and no tracking.

## What Scribe processes, and where

- **Your voice (microphone audio).** Audio is transcribed locally on your device
  by an on-device speech model. It is not uploaded to us and we never receive it.
  Audio is held only in memory during a recording and is discarded afterward.
- **Your transcripts.** Saved transcription history is stored **only on your
  device**. You can delete any item, or wipe everything, from Settings → Data.
- **Custom vocabulary and settings.** Stored locally on your device.

## Optional features that contact third parties

These are **off by default** and only run if you turn them on:

- **Cloud transcription (bring-your-own-key).** If you add your own API key and
  endpoint in Settings, the audio for that request is sent to **the provider you
  chose** (e.g. OpenAI), governed by that provider's privacy policy. Scribe does
  not see or proxy this traffic. Leave it off to stay fully offline.
- **Translation language packs (Google ML Kit).** The first time you translate
  into a language, the on-device translation model for that language is
  downloaded from Google's servers. This downloads model files only — your text
  is **not** sent anywhere; translation runs locally once the model is present.

## Device permissions

- **Microphone** — to hear what you dictate.
- **Speech recognition** — for on-device transcription.
- **Display over other apps** (Android, optional) — for the floating Flow Bubble.
- **Accessibility** (Android, optional) — only to paste dictated text into the
  field you're typing in. Scribe does not read, log, or transmit screen content.
- **Storage** — to save downloaded models and your local history.

## Data retention and deletion

All Scribe data lives on your device. Uninstalling the app, or using
Settings → Data → Delete all data, removes your history and downloaded models.

## Children's privacy

Scribe is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from them
(we don't collect personal data from anyone).
