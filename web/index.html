What it does

Everything Wispr Flow and Superwhisper do, without sending your voice anywhere.

Live dictation

Talk and watch text appear instantly, anywhere you can type. Punctuation and cleanup included.

New

Record mode

Capture a whole meeting or long note. It keeps recording in the background, then transcribes on stop.

New

Speaker labels

On-device diarization figures out who said what and lays the transcript out by speaker.

Translate, offline

Turn any transcript into 59 languages on demand. The translation models run on the device too.

Flow bubble & keyboard

A floating mic over any app, plus a full voice keyboard. Dictate into messages, notes, anything.

Pick your engine

From an instant built-in model to large multilingual ones. Choose by purpose, download once, run forever.

How it works

The audio never gets a chance to leave.

There's no upload step to opt out of. The model lives on your device, so your voice is processed where it's spoken.

01

You speak

The mic feeds audio straight into a model already downloaded to your device.

02

It transcribes on-device

Whisper / NeMo / your phone's own engine turns sound into text locally, even in airplane mode.

03

You keep the text

Edit, label speakers, translate, copy, or share. Nothing was ever sent to a server.
